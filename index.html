About Absolutely Delicious

We're your neighborhood spot for homemade comfort food. Every dish is prepared fresh daily using traditional recipes and quality ingredients. From fluffy pancakes to hearty soups, we take pride in creating meals that feel like home.

Located on Great Northern Road in Sault Ste Marie, we serve breakfast and lunch daily with convenient curbside pickup. Our frozen take-home meals let you enjoy our homestyle cooking anytime.
